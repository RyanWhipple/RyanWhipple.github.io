Hi, I'm Ryan, a Full-Stack Developer based out of the Phoenix area. I kept some of the world's largest organizations in motion during my careers in Air Force and data center logistics. In teams that large, success requires communication and that communication requires software. I was fascinated by the tools that shaped everything from users' daily work experience to achieving strategic goals.

As a leader I focused on equipping my teams with the tools they needed. This often meant collaborating with internal development teams, and sometimes it meant wireframing and diagramming new applications and workflows from the ground up. Building the foundations such big improvements was the most exciting part of the job!

With all my focus on designing software and processes, I realized it was time to make a career move and work more directly in those types of projects. I built on my existing skills by enrolling in a coding bootcamp, and I'm excited to continuing building my skills with the right company.
